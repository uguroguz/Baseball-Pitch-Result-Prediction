Rules

Part of rules that you need to know about baseball explained below. If you are interested in all of the baseball rules you can check it at Official Rule Book


Score Run

Baseball is a game that played with 2 teams, 40 players with 9 players take the field at any one time. The object of the game is to score more runs than the opposing team. To score a run a player must hit the ball between the foul lines and run across three bases and back home. A hit inside these lines is called as foul ball and batter is not allowed to run.

Strike

The pitchers job is to get batter out by throwing into strike zone.The strike zone is imaginary zone that is between batters armpits and knees. If the pitcher throws throug this area its strike.

Ball

The balls thrown outside of the strike zone called ball,if the batter swing and misses any ball it is also called strike. 3 strikes means batter is out. four balls against the batter and he gets to walk to first base.There severeal diffrent ways for team to get player out.

Go somewhere

The game is played over 9 innings,3 outs made in the 1/2 of the inning, ends the half of the inning. Other team gets to bat. Once both teams are batted this is known as inning.
